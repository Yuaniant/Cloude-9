ABOUT

White • Black • Calm

Clean lines, focused whitespace, subtle pixel texture — an aesthetic that nods to East-Asian minimalism and retro gaming vibes without becoming a literal game. This site is easy to edit and tablet-friendly. Use it to present your project and show off your team.

FEATURES

Minimal 白黒 UI

High contrast, readable, and intentionally minimal — white border gives it frame-like presence.

Pixel Texture

Delicate pixel-grid overlay for subtle retro feel — adjustable by changing CSS background-size on .page-wrap::after.

Animated Reveal

Sections fade in as you scroll — modern, elegant, and smooth for demos on tablet.

Discord Link

Quick join button for your community. Replace the invite URL in the code with your server's invite.
